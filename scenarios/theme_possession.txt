**THEME:** "The Host"
**CORE CONCEPT:** Not supernatural, but psychological. She develops a split personality or "Alter Ego" (The Bimbo) that starts taking over during blackouts.
**THE TRAP:** The Alter Ego is stronger, happier, and more sexual. The protagonist starts waking up in strange clothes with new surgeries she doesn't remember booking.
**KEY ELEMENT:** Losing time, waking up confused, finding notes left by the "other" self.
