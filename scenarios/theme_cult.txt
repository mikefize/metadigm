**THEME:** "The Inner Circle"
**CORE CONCEPT:** She joins an exclusive self-help group, MLM, or 'empowerment' sorority that promises success and happiness.
**THE TRAP:** The group uses love-bombing and sleep deprivation to brainwash her. "Success" is redefined as total aesthetic perfection and mental emptiness.
**KEY ELEMENT:** Smiling faces, toxic positivity, peer pressure, chanting.
