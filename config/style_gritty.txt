WRITING STYLE: Gritty & Grounded.
Write like a modern thriller author (e.g., Gillian Flynn or Chuck Palahniuk). Sentences should be sharp and punchy. Focus on the raw, uncomfortable reality of the situation. Describe the sweat, the ache in the muscles, the panic in the chest. Avoid all poetic or flowery language. Do not sound like a doctor; describe feelings as a person experiences them (e.g., "Her heart hammered," not "Her adrenaline spiked").
